Customer Journey Map

Prototype

Based on the obtained marketing data, an initial sample of users was made for in-depth interviews among the population of Switzerland, and later active users of Russia, as the most experienced users, in order to test a number of hypotheses and patterns.
CJM was created - the user's path was analyzed from the moment he received the initial information about the application and acquaintance with the sources, to the receipt of the ordered product, all the emotional and technical features of interaction with the MP at the main stages of the user path were identified.
Designed the main screens in the form of Wireframe, without design to demonstrate the logic and the overall concept to the customer, as well as for the initial tests with potential users of the future application, the detailed formation of the CJM.
The concept of redesigning the site of the famous Museum of Contemporary Art in Dubai was implemented within 2 weeks. The customer set the task of making the site as modern as possible with an emphasis on art and modern design. In the project, a special emphasis was placed on content. The client was satisfied with the preliminary concept and is ready to give recommendations.
